Time Managment base games

1.Diner Dash

The explosion of free time management games all started with a little game called Diner Dash. In Diner Dash, you take center stage as a waitress named Flo who bustles around a virtual diner, serving customers to collect cash. When you do it right, you get to advance to another level that's even more hectic.

Diner Dash is available on google play store

2.House Flip

House Flip with Chip and Jo is a simulation game that has a time management element. Based on a house-flipping television show, this game gives you plenty of opportunities to customize as you complete the tasks that get you closer to listing your renovated virtual house for sale.

House Flip is available on google play store

3.Elvenar

Elvenar is a time management game that draws on fantasy themes for its landscapes, characters, and tasks. You're building a magical city for elves and humans, crafting the items that you need for use or decoration from resources.

Elvenar games are available on google play store

4.Adelanto Trilogy

Set in the streets of Spain and the jungles of South America, Adelanto Trilogy is a time management game that starts with a quest to find and save a lost expedition. The game is set in the historical period when explorers were busy exploring new lands. In the game, you deal with everything from cave people to dinosaurs to scheming villains.

Adelanto Trilogy games are available on google play store .

5. Roads Of Rome

A fantasy/historical time management game, the Roads of Rome series motivates you to create and employ successful strategies to fix the roads and repair the settlements to restore the Roman Empire. With 43 levels of play, you'll eat up a lot of time before you get to the conclusion.

Roads Of Rome is avilable on google play store .
